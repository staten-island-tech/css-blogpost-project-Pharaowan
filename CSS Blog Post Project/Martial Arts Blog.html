Hand to Hand Combat

In the beginnning, humans did not know how to use objects as weapons, so they used their limbs as their weapons. From punches to kicks to all the manner of different strikes that exist today. This has continued to evolve to today with all the different hand to hand Martial Arts variations. From Karate, to Aikido to Kung Fu and of course Boxing.
